## 🔧 Complete Troubleshooting Guide <a id="troubleshooting" />

### 📶 WiFi Connectivity Issues (Most Common Problem)

**Problem:** Smart devices not connecting or frequently disconnecting

**Solutions:**
1. **Router Placement**: Central location, minimum 6 feet from ground, away from microwave/induction
2. **Network Separation**: Create separate 2.4GHz SSID for IoT devices (smart bulbs only work on 2.4GHz)
3. **Bandwidth Management**: Reserve bandwidth for smart home devices during peak hours
4. **Range Extenders**: Use WiFi extenders for large apartments (₹1500-3000 investment)

### ⚡ Performance & Hardware Issues

**Problem:** Slow response, flickering, or device malfunctions

**Solutions:**
1. **Internet Speed**: Minimum 2 Mbps dedicated for smart home devices
2. **Quality Components**: Replace cheap LED bulbs with branded ones (Philips, Osram, Havells)
3. **Voltage Stabilization**: Install voltage stabilizer in areas with power fluctuations (₹2000-5000)
4. **App Maintenance**: Clear cache weekly, update apps monthly, restart devices quarterly
