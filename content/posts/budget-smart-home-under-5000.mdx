## Buying Guide (Sara Confusion Dur Karo!)

### Smart Switch Types Samjho:
- **Touch Switches:** Modern kisi bhi wall ko stylish bana do, ek touch pe kaam shuru.
- **Retrofit Controllers:** Purane switchboards smart banao bina wiring badle.
- **Dimmer Switches:** Mood ke hisaab se light ki brightness set karo!
- **Modular Switches:** Existing boards mein seamlessly fit ho jate hain.

### Installation Ki Baat:
- **Neutral wire zaruri hai?** 2010 ke baad waale gharon mein mainly hai. Pehle check kara lo.
- **Internet band hua toh?** AZIOT jaise Bluetooth wala select karo – phone se control chalta rahega!
- **2.4GHz WiFi:** Sirf ispe hi setup hoga, 5GHz nahi chalega.
- **Load rating:** Light: 5A, Fan/AC: 10-16A minimum.

### Voice Control (Hindi Mein!)
- **Alexa:** "Alexa, bedroom light band karo", "fan chalu karo", "sab lights 50% karo"
- **Google:** "Hey Google, hall ki light dim karo", "kitchen wali light on karo"

## FAQs (Har Sawal Ka Jawaab)

**Q: Power cut mein chalega?**  
A: Inverter ya UPS pe toh smart switch chal sakta hai!

**Q: Ghar se door control?**  
A: Haan, app se pure India/world se control (internet chahiye)!

**Q: Bacchon ke liye safe hai?**  
A: Bilkul, koi exposed wire nahi, glass/ABS panel hota hai.

**Q: Wifi down?**  
A: Touch switch toh manually chalta hi rahega, Bluetooth option le toh phone se bhi direct control.

**Q: Bijli bill badhega kya?**  
A: Nahi, smart schedule & energy log se bill 15-40% kam hota hai!
